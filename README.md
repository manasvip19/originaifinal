# OriginAI

## Transforming Research Into Startup Opportunities

OriginAI is an AI-powered innovation intelligence platform that helps researchers, entrepreneurs, investors, universities, and innovation teams discover commercialization opportunities hidden inside research papers.

Instead of letting valuable discoveries remain locked in academic journals, OriginAI analyzes research, identifies real-world applications, evaluates market potential, and generates startup opportunities.

---

# Vision

Create a future where every meaningful research discovery has a clear path to real-world impact.

---

# Problem Statement

Millions of research papers are published every year.

Most of them never become products, companies, or solutions that improve people's lives.

Common challenges include:

* Researchers focus on discovery, not commercialization.
* Entrepreneurs struggle to discover valuable research.
* Investors struggle to identify emerging technologies early.
* Universities lack efficient technology-transfer processes.
* Industry often remains disconnected from cutting-edge research.

As a result, innovation moves slower than it should.

---

# Solution

OriginAI acts as an intelligent bridge between academia and industry.

The platform:

1. Understands research papers.
2. Extracts key discoveries.
3. Identifies industry applications.
4. Maps problems solved.
5. Generates startup concepts.
6. Evaluates market opportunity.
7. Produces investor-ready reports.
