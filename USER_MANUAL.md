# OriginAI User Manual

## Welcome

Welcome to OriginAI.

OriginAI helps transform research discoveries into startup opportunities.

---

# Getting Started

## Step 1

Create an account.

## Step 2

Log in to the dashboard.

## Step 3

Upload a research paper.

---

# Supported Inputs

* PDF
* DOI
* Research URL

---

# Research Analysis

After upload, OriginAI automatically:

* Reads the paper
* Extracts findings
* Creates summaries
* Detects innovations

---

# Discovery Explorer

View:

* Discoveries
* Technologies
* Research outcomes

Each discovery includes:

* Confidence score
* Innovation level
* Potential applications

# Industry Matches
