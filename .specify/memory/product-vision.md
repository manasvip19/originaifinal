# OriginAI Product Vision

One-liner
- Transform scientific research into actionable startup opportunities fast.

Target users
- Founders looking for high-potential technology ideas.
- Researchers seeking commercialization paths.
- Investors scouting novel tech with clear market fit.

Value proposition
- Convert papers (PDF, DOI, URL) into summarized discoveries, mapped industries, and ranked startup opportunities with an investor-ready briefing.

Success in 48h (MVP)
- Allow user to upload one paper, get a 1-page summary, 5 extracted discoveries, 5 mapped industries, 3 startup ideas, and a one-page investor report.

Longer-term vision
- Automated scouting across journals, continuous discovery feeds, and integration with deal pipelines.
